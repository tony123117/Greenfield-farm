About Greenfield Farm

Established in 1998, Greenfield Farm practices rotational grazing, compost-driven fertility, and crop diversity to create resilient food systems. We prioritize animal welfare and transparent practices.

At Greenfield Farm, we believe in sustainable and ethical farming practices. Our family-run farm is dedicated to raising livestock and growing crops in harmony with nature. We prioritize the health of our animals, the soil, and our community.
